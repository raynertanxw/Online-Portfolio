VizDayz
Not your typical countdown app!

I've always wanted to visualize how many days I have left to something I look forward to. Be it, graudation, end of a difficult post/assignment, or even just end of a chapter in my life, before the start of the next exciting one.

Something I've always felt amiss was that though I could see the number of days left, I could never really see how far I've come, or get a sense of how much time has already passed.

Hence I embarked to create this event visualiser for myself, and I share it free with everyone. This app is 100% free of in-app purchases and advertisements. A real no-nonsense app for free. Enjoy!
