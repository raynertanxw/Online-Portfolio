ShapeFlip

ShapeFlip is a puzzle arcade game revolving around matching shapes. The game tests playersâ€™ geometrical manipulation skills. A main shape is shown in the top middle of the screen with two options at the bottom left and bottom right. Players have to pick the matching shape and not get tricked by the other flipped shape. Time is a constraint as there are only five seconds given to figure out the matching shape.
